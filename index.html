Understanding the Basics

What is email marketing?

It's one of the most cost-effective ways to reach your customers ‚Äî and you don't need to be technical to use it.

Email marketing is simply the practice of sending emails to people who have opted in to hear from your business. Unlike cold outreach, your subscribers want to hear from you ‚Äî which is why it consistently outperforms other marketing channels.

You can use email to share news, promote products, nurture leads, or simply build a relationship with your audience over time. The best part? You own your email list. No algorithm can take that away.

Whether you run a small online shop, a freelance consultancy, or a local service business, email marketing can be set up in an afternoon ‚Äî and it scales with you for free.
